About Us

Who We Are

Giginov Professional Services Limited (Giginov) is a multi-competency enterprise transformation and technology-consulting firm that combines expertise and capabilities across many industries and business functions to deliver superior and agile value to its clients. Giginov collaborates with its clients, both in the private and public sectors, to build high performance enterprises.

Giginov principals and directors have a combined industry experience of over 28 years in various sectors of the economy: with competence in business transformation, financial advisory, software engineering, communication technology, e-Business development, government transformations (e-Government), systems integration, IT consulting, and process reengineering.

Giginov has deep understanding of the industries it works in, strong technical competencies and an outstanding track record both in the private and public sector. With its tested methodologies and best practices, its clients get best-fit, quality solutions that meet their business demands. With a flexible organization primed to deliver customized services to its clients, Giginov goes the extra mile to fully understand its clients real needs. Giginov offers its clients the capability to reinvent themselves and remodel their operations and business services using proven business methodologies.
